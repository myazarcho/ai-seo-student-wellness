# Step 10 — AI-Assisted Content Workflow

## Workflow Overview

This project uses AI to support marketing research and content production while keeping human review at every important decision point.

## Workflow

### 1. Define the Audience

Human task:
- Choose the target audience
- Define the platform boundary
- Identify realistic student problems

AI support:
- Suggest an initial audience persona
- Identify possible user needs

Human review:
- Remove assumptions
- Improve realism
- Confirm relevance

---

### 2. Generate Keyword Ideas

Human task:
- Define the topic areas
- Set safety boundaries

AI support:
- Brainstorm keyword ideas
- Suggest topic groups
- Suggest possible content formats

Human review:
- Remove irrelevant keywords
- Check safety level
- Select the most useful keywords

---

### 3. Analyze Search Intent

Human task:
- Understand the likely user problem

AI support:
- Classify keywords
- Suggest likely user expectations
- Recommend suitable content formats

Human review:
- Confirm whether the classification matches the keyword
- Adjust the article type when necessary

---

### 4. Build the Content Strategy

Human task:
- Decide the main content pillars
- Set publishing priorities
- Select the first article

AI support:
- Group keywords
- Suggest article titles
- Identify related topics

Human review:
- Remove duplicate ideas
- Improve pillar structure
- Select topics with clear audience value

---

### 5. Create the Article Outline

Human task:
- Define article goal
- Confirm search intent
- Set safety requirements

AI support:
- Suggest headings
- Create a logical sequence
- Suggest examples and FAQs

Human review:
- Ensure every section answers the user's need
- Remove unnecessary theory
- Add support-resource information

---

### 6. Draft the Article

Human task:
- Approve the outline
- Define tone and boundaries

AI support:
- Produce the first draft
- Suggest examples
- Improve structure

Human review:
- Edit unclear sentences
- Remove generic language
- Check realism
- Improve tone
- Confirm safety

---

### 7. Evaluate SEO Quality

Human task:
- Decide the evaluation criteria

AI support:
- Score the article
- Suggest SEO improvements
- Draft a meta description
- Identify internal-link opportunities

Human review:
- Confirm whether the recommendations are useful
- Reject changes that reduce clarity or safety

---

### 8. Evaluate Safety

Human task:
- Set the non-clinical boundary

AI support:
- Identify risky wording
- Suggest safer alternatives
- Check for overpromising and unsupported claims

Human review:
- Approve all final safety edits
- Confirm that the article does not replace professional support

---

## Final Workflow Model

Human Strategy
→ AI Assistance
→ Human Verification
→ Revision
→ Final Content

## Key Principle

AI improves speed and idea generation, but the human remains responsible for the final strategy, safety, and publishing decision.
