# Step 6 — Content Strategy

## Strategy Goal

The goal is to organize the keyword research into clear content pillars and create a practical publishing plan for a safe student mental-wellness platform.

## Content Pillar 1 — Assignment Management

### User Need

Students need practical ways to manage deadlines, prioritize tasks, and break large assignments into smaller steps.

### Target Keywords

- how to manage multiple assignment deadlines
- how to prioritize assignments
- study plan for multiple deadlines
- assignment planning for students
- how to break down a large assignment

### Article Ideas

1. How to Manage Multiple Assignment Deadlines Without Feeling Overwhelmed
2. How to Prioritize Assignments When Everything Feels Urgent
3. A Simple Study Plan for Multiple Deadlines
4. How to Break a Large Assignment Into Smaller Tasks

## First Article Selection

### Title

How to Manage Multiple Assignment Deadlines Without Feeling Overwhelmed

### Primary Keyword

`how to manage multiple assignment deadlines`

### Reason for Selection

This topic addresses a specific and common student problem. It has clear problem-solving intent, low clinical risk, and strong potential for practical content.

## Content Safety Rules

All content should:

- Avoid diagnosis
- Avoid medication advice
- Avoid therapy claims
- Avoid guaranteed outcomes
- Avoid blaming language
- Use realistic and supportive language
- Include university support options when appropriate
- Clearly state that the content is educational and non-clinical
