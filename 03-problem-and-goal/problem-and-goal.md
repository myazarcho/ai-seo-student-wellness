# Step 3 — Problem and Goal

## Problem Statement
University students often search online for help with deadlines, exams, time management, and academic pressure. However, some content is too generic, difficult to trust, or uses unsafe clinical language.

## Project Goal
To design an AI-assisted SEO content strategy that helps university students find safe, practical, and trustworthy information about academic stress and everyday student challenges.

## Portfolio Objective
To demonstrate skills in:
- Audience research
- Keyword research
- Search-intent analysis
- AI prompt design
- Content planning
- SEO writing
- Safety review
