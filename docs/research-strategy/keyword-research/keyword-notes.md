# Step 5 — Keyword Research Notes

## Research Goal

The goal is to identify search terms that university students may use when looking for practical, non-clinical support related to academic stress, deadlines, time management, exams, presentations, and university support services.

## Target Audience

- University students
- International students
- Master's students
- Students managing multiple assignments
- Students preparing for exams and presentations

## Primary Keyword

`how to manage multiple assignment deadlines`

## Why This Keyword Was Selected

This keyword represents a specific and realistic student problem.

The user is likely looking for practical steps rather than a general explanation of academic stress.

It also connects directly to the platform's goals:

- Academic workload support
- Time-management guidance
- Practical self-management
- Safe non-clinical content

### Academic Stress

## Search Intent Categories

### Informational

The user wants to understand a topic.

Example:

`what is academic stress`

### Problem-Solving

The user wants practical steps.

Example:

`how to prioritize assignments`

### Support-Seeking

The user wants emotional, academic, or professional support.

Example:

`feeling overwhelmed with university work`

### Navigational

The user wants to find a specific service or office.

Example:

`university counselling services`

## Safety Considerations

Keywords involving serious distress, diagnosis, medication, self-harm, or crisis situations require additional safety review.

This project focuses mainly on everyday academic stress and non-clinical student support.
